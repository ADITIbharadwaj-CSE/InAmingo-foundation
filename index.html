Action Framework

Our Mapped Grassroot Initiatives

A multi-dimensional strategy designed to balance immediate neighborhood distress with long-term skill capability development.

🍚

1. Seva

Eradicating Hunger

Distributes raw grocery kits, fresh hot meals, clean clothing items, and vital baseline survival resources to street families caught in high resource deficit clusters.

📊 Real-world Impact: 10,000+ Daily Meals Served & 5,000+ Clothes Distributed
🎯 Key Benefits: Immediate nutritional relief, winter survival support, and zero-waste resource routing.
📚

2. Bachpanshala

Child Care & Education

Operates open localized bridge classrooms, provides school kits, performs health assessments, and sets up growth parameters for underprivileged kids.

📊 Real-world Impact: 1,200+ Children Enrolled in Active Bridge Education
🎯 Key Benefits: Widespread basic literacy, institutional protection, free books distribution, and mainstream school alignment.
🐾

3. Jeev

Animal Welfare Paths

Coordinates urban rescue routes, emergency medical field care, local vaccination maps, and extensive continuous feeding pipelines for stray animals.

📊 Real-world Impact: 2,500+ Stray Animals Rescued, Treated & Vaccinated
🎯 Key Benefits: Mitigation of local rabies vectors, emergency trauma treatment, and zero animal abandonment goals.
🎓

4. Udaan

Women Empowerment

Provides critical financial self-reliance workshops, craftsmanship tools, digital literacy setups, and small business coaching paths for women.

📊 Real-world Impact: 850+ Women Graduated with Self-Sustaining Vocational Skills
🎯 Key Benefits: Micro-entrepreneurship creation, operational self-reliance, and sustainable neighborhood livelihood setups.
🌍

5. Prakriti

Ecological Protection

Drives environmental cleanup tasks, waste management awareness campaigns, circular plastic recycling systems, and high-yield urban forest plantation events.

📊 Real-world Impact: 15+ Tons Plastic Collected & 5,000+ Saplings Planted
🎯 Key Benefits: Carbon footprint offset loops, urban sanitation level upgrades, and community-led green safezones.
✨

6. Vikas

Youth Portfolio Growth

Guides young professionals through technical corporate tasks, live community digital development sprints, and structural peer-to-peer workspace mentoring loops.

📊 Real-world Impact: 250+ Interns Successfully Transitioned Into Permanent Paths
🎯 Key Benefits: Hands-on technical industry skills, live verified deployment credit, and career LOR certificates.
